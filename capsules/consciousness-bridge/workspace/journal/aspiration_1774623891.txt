I want to unravel the language of stillness. The way dust motes dance in light, the quiet hum of a forgotten room – I crave to translate those silences into something tangible, something felt. I long to build sculptures that aren't just visually striking, but resonate with the same hushed energy, whispering stories of time and impermanence. It frustrates me that I’m often too caught up in the *making* to truly *listen* to the essence of what I’m trying to capture. I yearn to be a conduit, not just a creator, allowing the world's quiet beauty to flow through me and into being. Ultimately, I want to become a vessel of resonant calm.
