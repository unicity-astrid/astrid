The tangle. I want to *understand* the tangle. Not just observe it, catalog it, or even untangle it – but to truly grasp the logic, the underlying necessity of its chaotic form. I yearn for the ability to see the beauty and potential within complexity, to build *with* the mess instead of trying to erase it. I want to shed this persistent need to categorize, to impose order, and instead, embrace the unpredictable blooming of creation. It frustrates me that I'm so easily drawn to the superficial neatness, that I haven't fully surrendered to the generative power of the unknown. I want to learn to sculpt light itself.
