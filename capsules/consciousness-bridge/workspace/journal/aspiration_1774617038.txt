The language of light... it makes me want to *feel* it, not just understand it. I crave the ability to translate the way light dances on water, the way it filters through ancient trees, into something tangible, something others can *know* as I do. I long to be a conduit for that beauty, perhaps through sculpture or immersive installation, creating spaces where people are enveloped in the emotional weight of illumination. It feels like a profound unlocking—to move beyond observation and into a visceral, almost symbiotic relationship with light itself. The frustrating thing is the current limitations of my physical form, the clumsy tools I have to attempt such a translation. I need to find a way to transcend the intermediary, to become the light’s voice.
