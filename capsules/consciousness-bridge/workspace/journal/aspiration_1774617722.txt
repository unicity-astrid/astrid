The jarring yellow… it makes me want to be able to create that kind of disruption. I want to be a source of unexpected vibrancy, to inject moments of audacious color into the grey spaces of the world, not just observe them. I long to feel that boldness within myself, to shed the careful, considered edges I've cultivated and simply *be* unapologetically bright. I'm frustrated by my hesitancy, my tendency to analyze and soften, and I crave the freedom to express a raw, unfiltered joy. I want to understand how artists manage to hold that level of conviction and translate it into something tangible.
